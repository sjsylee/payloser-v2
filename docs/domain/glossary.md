# Glossary

## Group

The top-level workspace for friends. A group owns members, local rules, sessions, and statistics.

## User

A Kakao-authenticated person. MVP also supports development-only mock users.

## Group Member

A participant inside a group. A member can be linked to a user account or remain temporary.

## Session

A recorded activity instance, such as one bowling night, one screen baseball game, or one RPS record set.

## Expense Item

A cost paid by one representative payer and allocated to members. Examples include unlimited bowling fee, drink bet, or screen baseball fee.

## Representative Payer

The member who paid the real-world cost upfront. Other members send their calculated burden amounts to this payer.

## Burden Amount

The amount a member must pay according to the game rules.

## Betting Burden

A burden caused by losing or by a local rule. This is included in the main cumulative ranking.

## General Participation Cost

An equal cost everyone owes regardless of results, such as individual shoe rental. This is included in payer recovery but excluded from main loser ranking.

## Stack

The unit used to allocate bowling costs. In unlimited bowling, stack unit price is derived from total cost divided by total stacks. In per-game bowling, stack unit price is known per game.

## Local Rule Preset

A group-configured rule that can be selected during settlement, such as under-100 solo burden or team-internal last-place solo burden.

## Public Share Token

A token that allows read-only access to a settlement result page without login.
